Have a spoon!

I was walking with number two son on Haight street, as is our habit, when kiddo finds a long handled spoon on the sidewalk. Compared to the usual broken glass and other unsavory what-not, it seemed like a perfectly reasonable treasure. A few doors down, the little rascal ran to the open window at Noc-Noc, threw the spoon in at two dudes, then ran away laughing.
